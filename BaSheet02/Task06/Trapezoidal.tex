\section*{Task 6}

The nodes in level $l$ are $\frac{i}{2^l}$ for $i=1,...,2^l-1$.
In level $l + 1$ they are $\frac{i}{2^{l+1}}$ for $i=1,...,2^{l+1}-1$.\\
Thus the level $l$ nodes are the even nodes in level $l+1$.
